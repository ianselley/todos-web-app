Welcome to my TO-DO's web app!

This is a productivity web where you can create notes (also called TO-DO's) and group them in categories.

Each note can have a description, an expiring date and even be marked as important.

Here you will learn how to use and move through this website.


First of all you have to create a new account. There you will have the option to log in automatically.

If you uncheck that option, you will need to log into the account you've just created.


Once logged in you will be on the My Categories section, where you can add as many categories as you want.
